On the Issues

A practical plan for a safer North County.

01 / Wildfire Prevention

Stop fires before they start.

Every year, North County families live with the fear of evacuation. We can do better by hardening the landscape and equipping our firefighters for the realities of today's fire season.

  • Clear hazardous brush and reduce fuel loads through thinning, grazing, prescribed burns, and new technology
  • Help partner agencies acquire a twin-engine firefighting helicopter, water tenders, and additional heli-hydrants
  • Conduct a full review of reservoirs, hydrants, and water mains to ensure they meet emergency needs
02 / Emergency Medical Services

When you call 911, help shows up fast.

Nearly 70% of North County Fire's calls are medical emergencies. No matter where you live in the district, you deserve fast, professional care when seconds count.

  • Maintain and improve one of the best EMS programs in the state
  • Invest in equipment, training, and technology that strengthens day-to-day readiness
  • Defend response times across every neighborhood we serve
03 / Community Resilience

Insurance, infrastructure, and coordination that hold up under pressure.

A resilient community is one where families can afford to live, where infrastructure is ready, and where every agency is rowing in the same direction when disaster strikes.

  • Push back against insurance carrier abandonment in fire-prone areas
  • Coordinate joint disaster planning between fire districts, water agencies, and emergency services
  • Strengthen large-scale readiness for the worst-case scenarios — before they happen
04 / Fiscal Responsibility

Transparency and discipline with every taxpayer dollar.

Public safety budgets are public trust. The board's job is to protect that trust, deliver results, and never confuse spending more with doing more.

  • Bring transparency to budgeting, contracts, and board decision-making
  • Invest in what works — and cut what doesn't
  • Treat the district's finances with the same care families do at the kitchen table
