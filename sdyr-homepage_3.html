— 01 / Mission

From North County to South Bay, we're a community of like-minded young professionals making San Diego a better place — for today and the generations after us.

Who We Are

SDYR is one of the most active chapters of Young Republicans in California. We're entrepreneurs, attorneys, veterans, students, and tradespeople — united by conservative values and a love for our city.

What We Do

We host monthly mixers, train candidates, knock doors, endorse races, and build the bench of conservative leaders for the next decade of San Diego politics.

— 02 / Pillars

→ 01

→ 03

Activate

Door-knocking, phone-banking, GOTV. The campaign trail is where movements are built — and where we live.

→ 04

Today's volunteer is tomorrow's councilmember. We train the next generation of conservative leadership in our county.
